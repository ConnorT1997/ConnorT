# Research Notes

## Overview
Collection of research memos, writing samples, and methodology documentation.

## Goal
This project serves as a repository for:
- **Research Memos**: Investment research notes and analysis
- **Writing Samples**: Professional writing examples
- **Methodology Documentation**: Research and analytical methodologies
- **Market Commentary**: Observations and insights on markets

## Tools & Technologies
- **Markdown**: Primary format for notes
- **PDF**: Published reports and documents
- **Version Control**: Track changes and evolution of ideas

## Status
Work in progress - research notes will be added over time.
